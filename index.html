Projects

OpenByLaws.org.za makes South Africa's by-laws freely available and easy to read and share. It transforms difficult to read, poorly scanned PDF files into webpages that are consistently and beautifully formatted, fully searchable and easy to link to. I was awarded a Code for South Africa Innovation Fellowship and a Shuttleworth Foundation Flash Grant for this project.

Wazi is a project to help journalists and others explore the demographics and political details of places around South Africa. The name comes from ulwazi, the Zulu word for knowledge, and the project is sponsored by Media Monitoring Africa.
